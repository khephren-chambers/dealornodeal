Output Descriptions

  • Professor Sentiment
    Assigned a category within a positive, neutral, or negative range corresponding to the results of sentiment analysis run on all student reviews of said professor
  • Course Sentiment
    Assigned a category within a positive, neutral, or negative range corresponding to the results of sentiment analysis run on all student reviews of the specific course, separate from feelings about the instructor
  • Holiday Assignments
    This metric represents how many assignments are due on holidays, off days, reading period, etc.
  • Grade Breakdown
    Gives the grade breakdown of a course given that the information is provided on the syllabus
  • Office Hours per Student
    A number that is the quotient of an instructor's office hours devided by the number of students enrolled in the course
