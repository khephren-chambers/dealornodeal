Deal or No Deal

UChicago Course Edition

Sis... dude... or whatever non-gendered term of endearment you prefer... WE KNOW.

We know you're tired of surfing through endless course evaluations. Posting all over social media. Mad add/dropping to get all the tea on those hot UChicago courses. Welcome to the place where you can find all the tea in one place. Period.

You might be wondering, "How did they get all this tea and what's the point of spilling it?"

We want a resource where students can find easily accessible and helpful information about courses or professors. Basically we compiled a database full of both syllabus information and course evaluations that can be accessed by YOUR input. Our fundamental aim for this project is returning power back to the people, so now you can be an informed consumer while you shop in the spicy world of UChicago course registration. To interact with our project, just click the tab "Get the tea..." at the top and search away!

TLDR; this is a database full of a bunch of messy (but now clean) CSV files mined from all over the world wide web (of uchicago.edu)
